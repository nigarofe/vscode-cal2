# Relações trigonométricas


## $\cos(A \pm B)$
$$\cos(A \pm B) = \cos A \cos B \mp \sin A \sin B$$

---
$$\cos(A+B) = \cos A \cos B - \sin A \sin B$$
$$\cos(\theta+90^\circ) = \cos \theta \cos 90^\circ - \sin \theta \sin 90^\circ$$
$$= \cos \theta \cdot 0 - \sin \theta \cdot 1$$
$$= -\sin \theta$$
---
$$\cos(A-B) = \cos A \cos B + \sin A \sin B$$
$$\cos(\theta-90^\circ) = \cos \theta \cos 90^\circ + \sin \theta \sin 90^\circ$$
$$= \cos \theta \cdot 0 + \sin \theta \cdot 1$$
$$= \sin \theta$$
