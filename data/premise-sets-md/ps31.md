## [Parametric equatio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parametric_equation)

$$x = a\cos(t)$$
$$y = a\sin(t)$$
$$\frac{x}{a} = \cos(t)$$
$$\frac{y}{a} = \sin(t)$$
$$\cos(t)^2 + \sin(t)^2 = 1$$
$$\bigl(\frac{x}{a}\bigr)^2 + \bigl(\frac{y}{a}\bigr)^2 = 1$$
$$x^2 + y^2 = a^2$$
